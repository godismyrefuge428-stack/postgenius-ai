## 2. Customer Acquisition (Automated)

### 2.1 Organic Channels (Minimal Work)

**SEO & Content Marketing:**
- Blog posts about AI marketing (1 per week)
- Optimize for keywords: "AI content generator", "social media automation"
- Target long-tail keywords with low competition
- Estimated traffic: 100-500 visitors/month by month 6

**Social Media (Autopilot):**
- Use PostGenius itself to generate content
- Schedule 5-7 posts per week
- Engage with comments (15 min/day)
- Join relevant communities (Reddit, Facebook groups)

**Community Presence:**
- Answer questions on ProductHunt, Indie Hackers
- Share wins and metrics (transparency builds trust)
- Participate in e-commerce communities
- Estimated conversions: 1-2 customers/month

### 2.2 Paid Channels (Scalable)

**Google Ads:**
- Budget: $500-$1,000/month
- Target: "content generation tool", "social media automation"
- Expected CAC: $150-$300
- Expected conversion rate: 2-5%

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Budget: $300-$500/month
- Target: E-commerce store owners, marketing managers
- Expected CAC: $100-$200
- Expected conversion rate: 1-3%

**Referral Program:**
- Offer $100 credit for each referral
- Customers get $50 off
- Incentivizes word-of-mouth
- Expected referrals: 5-10% of customer base

## 8. Scaling Strategy

### 8.1 When to Hire

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Solo**
- You handle everything
- Use automation extensively
- Estimated time: 20-30 hours/week

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Add Support**
- Hire part-time support person
- Cost: $2,000-$3,000/month
- Frees up 10 hours/week for you

**Phase 3 (Year 2): Add Product**
- Hire developer for new features
- Cost: $5,000-$8,000/month
- Accelerates growth

### 8.2 Outsourcing Opportunities

**Easy to Outsource:**
- Customer support (Zendesk + contractor)
- Social media management (use PostGenius!)
- Email marketing (Zapier + templates)
- Bookkeeping (Wave or accountant)

**Keep In-House:**
- Product vision
- Customer relationships
- Strategic decisions
- Quality control

## 10. Risk Management

### 10.1 Potential Issues

**Technical Risks:**
- API rate limits (OpenAI, Stripe)
- Database outages
- Payment processing failures

**Mitigation:**
- Set up monitoring and alerts
- Have backup providers
- Regular backups
- Redundant systems

**Business Risks:**
- Low conversion rate
- High churn
- Competition

**Mitigation:**
- A/B test landing page
- Improve onboarding
- Add unique features
- Build community

### 10.2 Contingency Plans

**If Conversion Rate is Low:**
1. Improve landing page copy
2. Add social proof (testimonials)
3. Lower price temporarily
4. Improve onboarding

**If Churn Rate is High:**
1. Survey churned customers
2. Improve product
3. Add customer success program
4. Offer discounts to retain

**If Costs Exceed Revenue:**
1. Optimize infrastructure
2. Reduce feature scope
3. Raise prices
4. Focus on higher-tier customers
